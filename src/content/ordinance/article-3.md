## Section 3-1 Lot Area {#section-3-1}

Minimum lot area standards apply to all principal uses.

## Section 3-2 Setbacks {#section-3-2}

Front, side, and rear setbacks must be met for new construction.

## Section 3-3 Building Height {#section-3-3}

Maximum building height is regulated by district.
