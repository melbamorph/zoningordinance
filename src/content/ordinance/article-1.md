## Section 1-1 Purpose {#section-1-1}

The purpose of this ordinance is to promote health, safety, and general welfare by regulating land use and development.

## Section 1-2 Authority {#section-1-2}

This ordinance is adopted under state enabling legislation and local charter authority.

## Section 1-3 Definitions {#section-1-3}

Key terms used throughout the ordinance are defined in this section.

### Subsection 1-3.A Applicability {#section-1-3-a}

Definitions apply to all articles unless otherwise stated.
